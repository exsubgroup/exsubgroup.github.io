⚙️ Watch Settings

Control which videos you get matched with. Shorter gaps mean fairer, more even trades — but fewer possible matches.

Create Video Tasks

The system will find members whose video length is close to yours (within your Allowed Gap) and create paired tasks automatically — one for you to watch their video, one for them to watch yours. Tasks are only generated when your pending count is below 5.

🎯 Members matching your Match Preferences (interests & countries) are prioritized first, then filled by closest video-length match. Adjust your to control video length range and gap tolerance.
