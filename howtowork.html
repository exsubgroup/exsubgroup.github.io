Step-by-Step Process

1
Create Your Account
Required ⏱ 2 minutes

Sign up with your username, password, and country. No email or gmail login required.

📝 Registration is quick & easy

How to register:

  • Open the account creation page from the sign-in screen.
  • Fill in your full name, choose a password, and select your country.
  • Double-check all details. And also you can change later in profile.
2
Setup Account
Required ⚙️ Add channels & videos

Add your promotional channel, videos, and create playlists. This is where you'll receive tasks and track progress.

📢 Add your promotional channel + videos + playlist

What to setup:

  • Go to the Channels page from the navbar.
  • Add your promotional channel (the one you want to grow).
  • Add videos to your channel for view and watchtime tasks.
  • Create a playlist if you want to join playlist watchtime groups.
  • Important: Add a working account — this is the YouTube account you'll use to complete tasks (subscribe, like, comment).
3
Install Exsub Extension
⭐ Essential 🔌 Proof sender extesnion

The Exsub Extension helps you track tasks, submit proofs, and stay organized. Install it and log in with your User Code.

🔌 Install the Exsub Extension now

How to install:

  • Go to the Exsub Extension page from the navbar.
  • Download and install extension in browser which support crx install like Lemur Browser.
  • Login in browser using your working gmail account with youttube channel for watching and subs work.
  • Login in extension with your Username , password – you're ready to go!
4
Join Any Group
Required 👥 Active community

Once your account setup complete, you can join any group you want and start working.

👥 Join your group and introduce yourself

How to join:

  • Go to the Groups page after your account setup complete.
  • Click any group join now and you will see you are joined
  • Go to the Tasks page Now you can work in Any task group.
✅
Things To Do
Follow these to stay in good standing
Exsub Extension is necessary to work in our group. Without this extension, you will not be able to send proof automatically to the admin.
You have to clearly check your task working, and subscribe and watch the right channel and videos using search.
Continuously working will lead you to a higher promotion in your group.
Contact support through the proper Support page. Avoid spamming and be patient while admins reply to you directly.
You don't need to worry about your watch time because our extension will send your proof automatically.
🚫
Things To Avoid
Violations may lead to suspension
Don't share your username and password.
Don't create multiple accounts because it will not help you at all.
Any kind of spamming in our group will lead to your removal from the group.
Subscribing and unsubscribing later will lead to your removal from the group.
Avoid delay in working tasks, especially pending tasks.
Avoid half completion of video watching because skipping is not allowed here.
