Step-by-Step Process

1
Register Your Account
Required 📝 Takes about 2 minutes

Sign up using the registration form. Provide your username, country, and contact details. Your account will be reviewed and assigned a unique User Code.

How to register:

  • Open the registration link shared by your referrer.
  • Fill in your full name, username, country, and WhatsApp number.
  • Double-check all details before submitting — errors will delay your approval.
  • After submission, wait for an approval message with your User Code.

Your User Code is your permanent ID inside Exsub Group. Keep it safe — you will need it for every task submission and support request.

2
Join Your Group
Required 👥 Check the Groups page

Once registered, you will be assigned to a group based on your region or role. Navigate to the Groups page and confirm your membership is active.

Finding your group:

  • Go to the Groups page after your account is approved.
  • Look for your name or User Code in the member list.
  • The group admin will send you an invite link to the correct channel.
  • Accept the invite and introduce yourself using your User Code.
3
Subscribe to Channels
Recommended 📢 Don't miss updates

Visit the Channels page to subscribe to relevant announcement and update channels. Staying subscribed keeps you informed about tasks and news.

Which channels to join:

  • Main Announcements — critical updates that affect all members.
  • Your Region Channel — tasks specific to your country or area.
  • Your Group Channel — direct communication with your group leader.

Turn on notifications for all subscribed channels so you never miss a time-sensitive task.

4
Complete Your Tasks
Ongoing ✅ Submit proof on time

Tasks are assigned through your group channel. Read each task carefully, follow the instructions exactly, and submit proof of completion when required.

Task workflow:

  • Read the full task description — including deadlines and proof requirements.
  • Complete the task exactly as described. No shortcuts.
  • Take a clear screenshot of the completed action as proof.
  • Submit your User Code + screenshot in the submission channel before the deadline.
  • Wait for the verification message before moving to the next task.
5
Report Issues
When needed ⚠️ Issues page is always open

If you encounter any problems — wrong task, payment issue, or technical error — go to the Issues page and submit a report with your User Code.

How to submit an issue:

  • Go to the Issues page from the navigation bar.
  • Select the issue category that best describes your problem.
  • Enter your User Code and a clear description of what happened.
  • Attach any relevant screenshots as evidence.
  • Submit the report and note your ticket number for follow-up.

The support team reviews all reports within 24 hours. Do not resubmit the same issue multiple times — it will not speed up the review.

🎯
Stay Active & Earn More
Bonus 🚀 Consistency is rewarded

Active members who consistently complete tasks and follow rules are prioritized for higher-value tasks and early access to new channels.

How to maximize your earnings:

  • Complete every task assigned to you — partial participation lowers your score.
  • Submit proof quickly. Fast submitters get priority in the next task round.
  • Refer trusted members — referral bonuses are paid after their first completed task.
  • Stay active in your group channel to be first to hear about bonus tasks.
✅
Things To Do
Follow these to stay in good standing
Always use your registered User Code when submitting reports or tasks.
Read all task instructions fully before starting. Mistakes due to skipping instructions are not reimbursed.
Submit clear screenshot proof when required. Blurry or cropped images will be rejected.
Stay subscribed to your assigned channels to never miss task announcements.
Contact support through the proper Support page. Avoid DMing admins directly.
🚫
Things To Avoid
Violations may lead to suspension
Do not share your User Code or account access with anyone else.
Do not submit fake or edited screenshots as proof of task completion.
Do not create multiple accounts. Each member is allowed one account only.
Do not spam the channels or group with repeated messages or off-topic content.
Do not attempt to claim tasks that were not assigned to you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Account approval typically takes between 24 to 48 hours. You will receive a notification once your account is active and your User Code has been assigned.
Missed deadlines cannot be extended retroactively. Make sure to check your channels regularly. If you had a genuine emergency, submit an issue report explaining the situation and it will be reviewed.
Payments are processed after task verification. The payment method and schedule are specified in each task's instructions. Make sure your payment details are correctly registered in your profile.
By default, each member is assigned to one primary group. If you believe you qualify for multiple groups, contact support with your User Code and they will review your eligibility.
Suspended accounts can submit an appeal through the Issues page within 7 days of suspension. Include your User Code and a clear explanation. Repeat violations may result in a permanent ban.
